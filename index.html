About Us

Mission: To build climate-smart, energy-efficient, and economically empowered communities that emit fewer greenhouse gases than they absorb.

Vision: A future where every African community is energy secure, climate resilient, and economically self-sufficient.

We replace firewood and diesel with clean alternatives like solar mini-grids, electric mobility, solar-powered cooling, and smart agriculture tools to build holistic, sustainable villages.

Our Focus

A. Energy Access (Climate Mitigation)

Goal: 1 million people with clean energy by 2030 through solar mini-grids and battery packs.

B. Climate Adaptation

Post-harvest loss reduction via solar cold storage; solar-powered EVs for medical and supply logistics.

C. Capacity Building

Train 1,000 youth annually via NZV Academy. Use digital tools like IoT and mobile tracking.

D. Advocacy & Research

Policy advocacy, rural innovation, and partnerships with research institutions.
